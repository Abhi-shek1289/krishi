Fertilizers

Fertilizers- Nitrogen (N) Nitrogen fertilizers such as urea, ammonium sulfate, and ammonium nitrate are commonly used for wheat cultivation. The recommended nitrogen application rates vary depending on the stage of growth, but typically range from 60 to 120 kg/ha. Phosphorus(P) Phosphate fertilizers like diammonium phosphate (DAP) and monoammonium phosphate (MAP) are commonly used for wheat cultivation.The recommended phosphorus application rates vary depending on soil test results, but typically range from 30 to 60 kg/ha Pottasium(K) Potash fertilizers such as muriate of potash (MOP) and sulfate of potash (SOP) are commonly used for wheat cultivation. The recommended potassium application rates vary depending on soil test results, but typically range from 30 to 90 kg/ha.
