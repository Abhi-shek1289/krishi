Pesticides

Pesticides- Herbicides Common herbicides used in wheat cultivation include glyphosate, 2,4-D, and dicamba. The choice of herbicide depends on the weed species present and the stage of wheat growth. Insecticide Common insecticides used in wheat cultivation include pyrethroids, neonicotinoids, and organophosphates. The choice of insecticide depends on the type of insect pest and the severity of infestation. Fungicides Common fungicides used in wheat cultivation include triazoles, strobilurins, and azoles. The choice of fungicide depends on the specific fungal disease present and the stage of wheat growth.
